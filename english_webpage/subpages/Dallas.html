Attractions in Dallas

Reunion Tower

Photo by Yassin Nkya on Unsplash

The reunion tower is 561 feet (170 meters) tall observation tower in Dallas. The tower is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the city. They even have a restaurant on the top of the tower where you can experience rare steaks or fine seafood.

Klyde Warren Park

Photo by Kelly Sikkema on Unsplash

Named for the billionaire son that donated 10 million dollars to develop the park it is located in downtown Dallas, Texas. With the open space, food trucks and loads of activities it is a family friendly experience.

Dallas Zoo

Photo by Cliff on Unsplash

Dallas Zoo is a 106-acre zoo located south of downtown Austin. The zoo was established in 1888 which makes it the oldest​​ zoological park in Texas. The Dallas Zoo is home to over 2000 animals from 406 different species.

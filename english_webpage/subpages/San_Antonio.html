Attractions in San Antinio

The Alamo

Photo by Lori Stevens on Unsplash

The Alamo is a historic fortress compound founded in the 18th century. This is where the battle of the Alamo took place in 1836, An important event in the Texas revolution./p>

San Antonio River Walk

Photo by Yeshwarya Pakki on Unsplash

You have to see this 15-mile waterway that travels through the heart of the Alamo City. You read that right, 15 miles – it is full of incredible restaurants, unique boutiques, and phenomenal nightlife options, there’s so much more to explore here! If you come to Texas you really should consider going there for a visit and enjoy it all!

San Antonio zoo

Photo by Daiga Ellaby on Unsplash

Open from 9 to 5 this is the zoo for you. It is in midtown san antonio There is everthing you will expect from a zoo, in excess of 8500 animals including elephants,giraffes and pelicans. Some of the animals are also examples of animals on the endangered species list. It was officially established in 1914.
